Design Overview

Product Highlights & Key ICs

The 1.6T OSFP SR8 leverages a COB assembly design, 400G angled lens technology with optimized reflection and insertion loss balance, and full automatic assembly & test capability.

Product Highlights
  • 200G per lane Electronic/Optical signal processing
  • Power consumption <32W total module budget
  • Supports Dual MPO-12 breakout (2×800G) application
  • Mature COB design with optimized thermal management
  • 400G angled lens design — balanced reflection & insertion loss
  • Automatic assembly and testing workflow
  • Supports both AOC and MPO pluggable configurations

Design Rationale
  • Doosan DS-8502SQN MSAP substrate — recommended for consistent performance
  • Low Dk and low-loss tangent — ideal for 200G/lane PAM4 channels
  • 16-layer count ensures consistent via-to-via alignment at all layer transitions
  • Impedance design: GF 100Ω → GF-CAP 97Ω → CAP-DSP 95Ω (Host)
  • Line-side: DSP Pad 100Ω → DSP-CAP 90Ω → CAP-LDD 86Ω
